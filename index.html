FIELD OF COMMAND

OPERATIONAL WWII STRATEGY

Lead a British company from the retreat at Dunkirk to the Rhine crossing in this operational-scale RTS. Experience the "Mission Command" system where you orchestrate maneuvers through a realistic hierarchy of AI subordinates, manage physical supply lines, and preserve a veteran core of troops and commanders across a persistent WWII career.

PLAY PROTOTYPE WISHLIST ON STEAM

THE LIVING CANVAS

Experience the war through a "painterly diorama" aesthetic. The world renders like a moving oil painting, transitioning seamlessly from a paper reconnaissance map to the mud of the battlefield.

COMMAND THROUGH INTENT

Issue orders through a command hierarchy. Draw objectives and watch your AI subordinates interpret and execute them through Brigade → Battalion → Company levels. You set the plan; your deputies handle the tactics.

SPICE LOOP: Scan  |  Prioritize  |  Intervene  |  Commit  |  Evaluate

LOGISTICS AS GAMEPLAY

Supply is physical infrastructure, not an abstract resource. Protect your Main Supply Routes while severing the enemy's. Deploy the Royal Service Corps or Royal Engineers to build dumps, bridge rivers, and establish clearing stations.

BUILD A VETERAN FORCE

Your career spans the entire war. Rise from Captain to Lieutenant-General. Units and Commanders that survive gain experience and traits. That tank crew from the desert carries their skills into Italy if you keep them alive.

THEATRE-SCALE WARFARE

Command operations spanning entire theatres of war. Thousands of simulated units across maps measured in hundreds of miles. From managing a single company at Dunkirk to coordinating corps-level offensives across North-West Europe.

CAMPAIGNS: Dunkirk, North Africa, Sicily, Italy, Normandy, Arnhem, Rhine.
