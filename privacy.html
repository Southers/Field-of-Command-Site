Privacy Protocols

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Identity

This terminal is operated by the developers of Field of Command.

2. Intelligence Collection

We only collect the following data when you voluntarily enlist:

  • Email Address: Collected via our sign-up form for the Field Report newsletter.
  • Cookies: Used for anonymous operational analytics (Google Analytics) to improve mission parameters.

3. Operational Use

We use your email address solely to transmit:

  • Development updates and "Field Report" newsletters.
  • Invitations to Alpha/Beta playtests.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to enemy factions or third parties.

4. Third-Party Infrastructure

We use the following secure channels:

  • Cloudflare: Hosts our infrastructure and database.
  • Google Analytics: Analyzes site traffic (anonymized).

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR protocols, you have the right to:

  • Discharge: Unsubscribe at any time via the link in our emails.
  • Erasure: Request total deletion of your data records.
